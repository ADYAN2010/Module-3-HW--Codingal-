Our Secret Recipe

Ingredients:
- 2 liters of fresh milk
- 500g sugar
- 1 tsp cardamom powder
- Ghee for greasing

Instructions:
1. Boil the milk until it thickens.
2. Add sugar and stir continuously.
3. Mix in cardamom powder for flavor.
4. Shape into desired forms and let cool.
